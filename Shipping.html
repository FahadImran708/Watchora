Shipping Policy

Effective Date: August 3, 2025

Thank you for shopping with OurWatches. Below you’ll find details about how we handle shipping and delivery.

1. Shipping Locations

We currently ship to most countries worldwide, including:

  • United States
  • Canada
  • United Kingdom
  • Pakistan
  • Europe and select international locations

2. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after payment is received. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

3. Shipping Time

Estimated delivery times:

  • USA: 5–10 business days
  • International: 7–20 business days (varies by country and customs)

4. Shipping Fees

We offer Free Standard Shipping on all orders. Expedited shipping may be available for an additional fee.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you’ll receive a confirmation email with tracking details. Please allow 24–48 hours for the tracking link to update.

6. Customs & Import Duties

International customers may be subject to customs fees, duties, or taxes imposed by their country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer.

7. Delays

We are not responsible for shipping delays caused by weather, carrier issues, or customs clearance. We appreciate your patience.

8. Wrong or Incomplete Address

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We are not liable for lost packages due to incorrect or incomplete addresses.
